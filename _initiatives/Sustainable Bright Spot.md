<h3>Sustainable Bright Spot&nbsp;</h3>
<p>The Sustainable Bright Spot is a long-term programme that encourages residents
of housing estates to embrace the value of picking-up after themselves.</p>
<p>In a Sustainable Bright Spot, residents take strong ownership of the cleanliness
of their neighbourhood. There is a culture where residents themselves are
empowered to be advocates - within their community and are involved in
regular activities and awareness programs that promote cleanliness of shared
public spaces.&nbsp;
<br>
<br>Download this playbook to find out how to activate your community to be
a Sustainable Bright Spot.&nbsp;</p>
